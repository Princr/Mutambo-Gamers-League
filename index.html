MUTAMBO

GAMING LEAGUE

We are a community of game developers, game enthusiasts and gamers who are all brought together by the love of the game. Our mission is simple, to grow the gaming and e-sports industry in the region by bringing together the best and most skilled gamers together.

We host free to enter and game pass oriented tournaments and LAN parties for all competitive gamers of any skill level and across different platforms. It is time that we took gaming seriously and join the thousands of gamers who generated over a hundred billion dollars in revenue in 2017.

We are serious about games. Its not just all fun and games, we are here and in it for the money, it is time that gamers made real dollars from their skill! Our first step in connecting the gaming community is setting up a leaderboard for gamers who participate in tournaments. The highest ranking players will be able to cash out points for cash and rewards, backed by our corporate sponsors. Here is the exciting part. Anyone can host a tournament. The tournament should simply have more than 30 registered gamers and have a Mutambo League representative present for the tournament. Ours is an African narrative, we believe in supporting locally developed games that tackle challenges and are built on environments that we can relate to. It is for this reason that we believe that building our community of gamers, will complement that Game development community that is growing in Zimbabwe.

We believe that the way we work and the way we play ultimately shapes our communities and societies and are cognizant of the fact that, the road that lies ahead is riddled with challenges. So here is the thing, we believe in this grand vision and will do whatever it takes to see it through. The question is, will you join us? Help us see this through.
